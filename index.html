Mohinga

Mohinga is a beloved dish in Myanmar and can be enjoyed as a breakfast, lunch, or snack food. The dish has a rich cultural history and has been a staple in Burmese cuisine for centuries.

Mohinga is a popular dish from Myanmar, with a history that dates back to the 12th century. It's a fish-based soup with rice noodles and herbs, often eaten for breakfast and considered the national dish of Myanmar.

Calories: 300-500
Carbohydrates: 50-80g

Shan Noodles is one of the most popular dishes in Myanmar and can be enjoyed as a breakfast, lunch, or snack food. The dish has a rich cultural history and has been a staple in Burmese cuisine for centuries.

A traditional dish of the Shan people, Shan Noodles have been a staple in the Shan State region of Myanmar for generations. The recipe typically includes rice noodles, meat, and a flavorful sauce.
